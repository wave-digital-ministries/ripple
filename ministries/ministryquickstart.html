Quick Start a Ministry

A ministry is not just large organizations or churches.

A ministry can be as simple as sharing Bible verses on social media (This is a lot of what we do). However, where do you start when starting a ministry?

First, find a Bible translation that you like and can easily share. A
great tool for finding a translation you like and sharing from is the Bible App from YouVersion.

Second, select a name for your ministry. Find something as unique as
possible. A good tool for finding out what names have been used is
Google or DuckDuckGo.

Fourth and last step, start ministering. Remember to be consistent and faithful in ministering.
