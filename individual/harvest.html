The Harvest

"You will reap whatever you sow."

This is true in all ways of life (Christian or not). If you plant bad seeds you will either not reap at all, or reap bad produce. If a business man spends hundreds of dollars on drinks for his business and neglects marketing bills, his business will collapse. This is of course a modern day example. We need to make sure that our good "seeds" are things like what we do with our time and who we spend our time with.
