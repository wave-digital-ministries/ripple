Snippet: The Harvest

You will reap whatever you sow." This is true in all ways of life (Christian or not). If you plant bad seeds you will either not reap at all, or reap bad produce. If a business man spends hundreds of dollars on drinks for his business and neglects marketing bills, his business will collapse. This is of course a modern day example. We need to make sure that our good "seeds" are things like what we do with our time and who we spend our time with.

"As I have observed, those who plow evil and those who sow trouble reap it."

Job 4:8 NIV

"A wicked person earns deceptive wages, but the one who sows righteousness reaps a sure reward."

Proverbs 11:18 NIV

What are you sowing?

Snippet: All It Takes Is a Ripple

God has used individual people a lot. He used Moses to free His people. Moses, one individual man (With God's help and blessing) freed all of the Isrealites from Egypt. Moses was a murderer. Yet, God still used him. God didn't let Moses' past interfere with his future.

This means that God can use anyone for His Kingdom. He doesn't care about your background, He cares about your future.

All it takes is one person ministering to change many people's lives. God changing people's lives is just like a ripple. Once it starts it keeps going into the space around them.

Are you going to be that ripple?
