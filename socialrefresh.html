Social Refresh

As I was taking this week to get off of social media, I was taken back on how much you miss life if you live on the touch screen all day.

When I was a kid social media was never any of our thoughts in life. We were ever-present in our current situation whether it was school, vacation, or just being at home. It’s a moment like those you were just living life not performing or projecting life constantly. Don’t get stuck on a routine of updating status but instead update in real life. Instead of jumping onto Facebook in the morning before you even brush your teeth try and actually Book to meet someone’s Face. ( it was a pun not a good one) encountering others and revealing how life is and being real about current situations in life of how we’re struggling in our faith or how jobs being stressful or you feel you are in a season of trial and you need to lean on some support to help you through. The Bible talks about a friend standing closer than a brother or sister when times of adversity come and it is hard to find those people when we refuse to engage cause it might be embarrassing or we don’t want others to think less of us. The enemy traps us in that snare telling us a lie that telling others about how life is and being honest will ruin friendships and show people how we have nothing. But scripture says in Proverbs 27:17 NLT “As iron sharpens iron, so a friend sharpens another friend. It is best to hear the most truthful advice from someone who genuinely has the best interest for us. I don’t mean yes men. I mean honest people who will tell you what the actual situation is and not look to offend you at the same time not to coddle you. A few verses before this previous scripture it says “The heartfelt counsel of a friend is as sweet as perfume and incense.” Proverbs 27:9.

So be intentional in life and in getting to know a friend. Don’t let occasional pictures and page bios keep you from reaching out to a friend and asking them how they're doing. Cause often times the focus of conversation won’t be on their recent post but of current life goals or obstacles they are currently facing. God loves a community under him reaching out toward others
