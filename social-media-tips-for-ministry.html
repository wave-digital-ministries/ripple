Social Media Tips for Ministry

A social media presence is important for ministry.

A social media presence is important, especially during the COVID-19 outbreak. When making posts, make sure that they are relevant hashtags. Otherwise, they will not be seen very much. Let's tak a look at a few hashtags that have worked well for us.

#beawave4christ #jesus #jesussaves #jesuslovesyou #biblestudy #ministry #ministrylife #inspiration #motivation #life #christian #bible #bibleverse

All of these hashtags have to do with our content. We normally add additional tags to improve the likelihood of increased views. The hashtags will help your ministry's reach.
