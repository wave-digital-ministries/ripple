Worry

The world is currently filled with fear, worry, and doubt.

The world is changing constantly as COVID-19 sweeps across the globe,
but there is one thing that will never change: God is on the throne. God
has not abandoned us. He is helping us though this trial. He has given
us prayer so that we can come to Him whenever and now is no different.

We are to bring our struggles and worries to God. He loves us and cares for us.

Discussion Questions 

What should we do with our worries?

Can you think of any other verses about worry and fear?
