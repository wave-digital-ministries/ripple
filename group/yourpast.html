Your Past Does Not Prevent Your Future

Many people believe that their past separates them from God’s love and redemption. If this was true, no one would go to Heaven. God will take your past and give you a future. In Acts 9 we read that Jesus redeemed a murderer who was killing Christians.

Jesus came to Saul and instructed him to change his ways. Jesus then called Saul to ministry.

Jesus did not care about Saul’s past, because He could see Saul’s future.

Jesus sees your future too. All you need to do is believe in Him.

Discussion Questions 

Can Jesus save us even though we have sinned?

Have you asked Jesus to save you from your sins?

How can we tell other people learn about God's grace?
